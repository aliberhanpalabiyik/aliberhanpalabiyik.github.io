Welcome to my personal math page

I am an undergraduate mathematics student at the University of Bonn, currently interested in mathematical logic and model theory. On this site I collect selected notes, small expository texts, and some thoughts on studying mathematics.

(TR) Bu siteyi hem master başvuruları için akademik bir profil olarak hem de kendi çalışma notlarımı ve yazılarımı düzenli bir yerde toplamak için kullanıyorum.

What I am currently working on

  • Foundations of analysis and sequences/series of functions
  • Linear algebra, kernels & images, and change of basis
  • First steps into mathematical logic and model theory
  • Improving proof-writing and problem-solving habits

Notes & materials

I keep cleaned-up versions of some of my lecture notes and self-study summaries here:

  • Analysis – limits, continuity and series
  • Linear Algebra – kernels, images, and rank
  • Short LaTeX projects and cheatsheets

Most notes are written in LaTeX and exported as PDF. Over time, I plan to add more polished documents and short expository pieces.
